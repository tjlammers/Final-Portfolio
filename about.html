About Me

I am an Iowa native, growing up in a the Ames area. Although I lived in Cyclone country, I decided to pursue my college education in Iowa City. I enrolled at the University of Iowa in the fall 2020 semester. Originally, I came to Iowa as an Industrial Engineering major. After my first semester I started to develop a love for statistics through sports, and wanted to make that my future career. I changed my major to Business Analytics and Information Systems, which I partnered with a minor in Sport Recreation Management. I am graduating with my B.B.A. in May 2024. Next fall, I plan to futher my education by pursuing a M.B.A. from the University of Nebraska-Omaha.

My first position was a Student Coordinator for the Parking and Transportation team. In addition, I worked as a Teaching Assistant for Financial Management 3000 during the Fall 2023 semester.
