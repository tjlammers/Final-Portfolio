About Me

Growing up in the Ames area of Iowa, I was immersed in the Cyclone culture. However, when it was time to choose a college, I opted for Iowa City. In the fall of 2020, I enrolled at the University of Iowa, initially pursuing a degree in Industrial Engineering. Over time, my interests shifted towards statistics, particularly in sports. This newfound interest led me to switch my major to Business Analytics and Information Systems, with a minor in Sport Recreation Management. I'm set to graduate in May 2024 and plan to pursue a Master of Business Administration at the University of Nebraska-Omaha in the fall. In addition to my studies, I've gained valuable professional experience. In the summer of 2023, I interned as a Project Coordinator at F&G Life in Des Moines, where I collaborated with experienced professionals. During my time in Iowa City, I held various roles within the university, including coordinating parking and transportation services and serving as a Teaching Assistant for Financial Management 3000. As I prepare for the next chapter in my life, I'm eager to apply my skills and knowledge in new endeavors.
